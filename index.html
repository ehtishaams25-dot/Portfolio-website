about me

My journey started back in school editing on my phone with PicsArt. Then one random day I discovered Adobe Photoshop and that pretty much changed everything.

What started as casually making edits turned into an obsession with visuals, storytelling, motion, and content creation. I began with graphic design, but naturally shifted toward video editing, especially long-form YouTube content where pacing and storytelling matter more than flashy transitions every two seconds.

Over the last 3+ years, I’ve worked with 17+ brands across different industries, creating branding, motion design, short-form content, and long-form videos that have generated 50M+ combined views across platforms.

Today, I focus on creating content that feels clean, intentional, engaging, and memorable.
